Akka.NET and Async Await

To enable Async Await support in an Akka.NET actor, you will need to configure your arctor to use the task-dispatcher. This can be accomplished like this:

var actor = Sys.ActorOf(Props.Create<AsyncAwaitActor>()
           .WithDispatcher("akka.actor.task-dispatcher"));

When the task-dispatcher is active, every incoming message to the actor will first be processed through the dispatcher itself. The dispatcher will apply the "async state" that is needed for async await operations to work.
